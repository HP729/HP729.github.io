Keyboard Navigation Guide

To navigate with a keyboard, press Tab. You can then press:

  • Tab: Move to the next link or button
  • Shift + Tab: Move to the previous element
  • Enter: Activate a button or link
  • Up Arrow Key: Navigate upwards between links and buttons
  • Down Arrow Key: Navigate downwards between links and buttons
  • Spacebar: Activate buttons

Usability Focus: I incorporated visibility by making my website highlight where you are on the website. Consistency is also practiced as you can use tab and up and down arrow keys to navigate the the entire site, you can click on anything by using enter key. This cconsistency allows for easy use of my website.
