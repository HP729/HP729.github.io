AMD Graphic Cards

Currently on RDNA4, AMD continues to push the limits of gaming.

AMD's current GPU's: the 9070 & 9070XT were a success. With the 9060XT releasing soon.

Popular AMD GPU Generations

  • Radeon RX 500 Series

    • The RX 500 Series was released in 2017. It offered great value and performance.

  • Radeon RX 5000 Series (RDNA 1)

    • The RX 5000 Series was released in 2019. It was the first AMD GPU to use the RDNA architecture.

  • Radeon RX 6000 Series (RDNA 2)

    • The RX 6000 Series was released in 2020. It was the second generation of the RDNA architecture.

  • Radeon RX 7000 Series (RDNA 3)

    • The RX 7000 Series was released in 2022. It was the third generation of the RDNA architecture.
